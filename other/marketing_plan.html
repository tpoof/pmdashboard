Overview

Marketing creates demand, assets, positioning, and measurable adoption. Announcements are not the strategy.

Annual Objectives

  1. Increase Facility-Level LEAF Adoption
  2. Reduce Time from Awareness to First Workflow
  3. Position LEAF as a Modernization Enabler at Enterprise Scale
  4. Equip Facilities with Reusable Marketing Assets

Quarterly Plan (Structured by FY)

Q1 (Oct–Dec): Foundation & Positioning

Objective

Establish clear positioning and create reusable enterprise marketing assets.

Deliverables
  • Develop a clear LEAF value proposition statement
  • Create a one-page Facility Infographic or Flyer
  • Create a LEAF Overview Slide Deck facilities can reuse
  • Build a centralized Marketing Assets Page inside LEAF
  • Create standardized talking points for leadership briefings
Outcomes

Facilities can self-serve marketing materials.

Success Indicators

Asset requests/downloads increase month over month.

Q2 (Jan–Mar): Activation Campaign

Objective

Turn interest into launched workflows.

Deliverables
  • Launch "Launch in 30 Days" campaign
  • Publish a First Workflow Starter Kit
  • Develop preconfigured workflow templates
  • Host targeted facility onboarding sessions
  • Create a before/after workflow case example
Outcomes

Reduce time to first workflow by X percent.

Success Indicators

Increase in first-time workflow launches quarter over quarter.

Q3 (Apr–Jun): Enterprise Visibility Push

Objective

Increase high-visibility exposure at facility, regional, and national levels.

Deliverables
  • Present LEAF in at least X regional leadership forums
  • Secure inclusion in at least one national modernization briefing
  • Publish two facility success stories with measurable outcomes
  • Develop a short executive impact summary
Outcomes

Expand into new facilities/program offices.

Success Indicators

Number of new facilities adopting LEAF this quarter.

Q4 (Jul–Sep): Expansion & Proof of Impact

Objective

Demonstrate measurable modernization impact and drive expansion.

Deliverables
  • Publish annual impact summary
  • Quantify estimated hours automated or workflows digitized
  • Identify top performing use cases
  • Develop roadmap preview for next fiscal year
  • Conduct listening sessions with high-usage facilities
Outcomes

Increase workflows per active site by X percent.

Success Indicators

Workflow volume growth quarter over quarter.
