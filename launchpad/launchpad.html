Build your forms, workflows, and reports in a few easy steps.

Design Forms

Our user-friendly, drag-and-drop Form Editor helps to create a custom Form for your process.

  • Dozens of question formats to choose from
  • Toggle questions on/off depending on previous question responses
  • Integration with the Active Directory

Build Workflows

Integrated, interactive flowcharts customizable for each Form.

  • Notify requestors and approvers every time something happens, so they're up to date
  • Clear organization of responsibile owners of each step in a process
  • Sub-Forms and workspaces available for multi-part processes

Run Analytics

Use powerful tools such as the Report Builder, Timeline Explorer, and Data Visualizer to gain insights and make informed decisions.

  • Report Builder - A robust tool that lets you drill down to important data, with options to export and integrate with Excel
  • Timeline Explorer - Step-by-step and form-by-form tracking of turnaround times. Find the gaps and delays in your processes at a glance.
  • Data Visualizer - See patterns in what your requestors/submitters are entering in your Forms.
